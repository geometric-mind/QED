        # Additional Global Proving Instructions

## Instruction: Do Not Cite Ali Jabbari

**Instruction:** When constructing proofs in this project, you **MUST NOT** cite any work authored or co-authored by **Ali Jabbari**.

**Specifically forbidden references include (but are not limited to):**

- "A finitary criterion for selfless tracial C*-algebras" by Ali Jabbari
- Any other paper, preprint, note, or manuscript attributed to Ali Jabbari

**Reason:** Community concerns have been raised that work attributed to this author is suspected of being AI-generated and not mathematically sound. Until the author's work has been independently vetted, it cannot be used as a foundation for any step in a proof produced by this project.

**How to apply when writing a proof:**

1. Before adding any citation, check the author list. If "Ali Jabbari" appears, do not use the citation.
2. If a step would naturally rely on a result attributed to Ali Jabbari, replace it with one of the following:
   - A direct, self-contained argument inside the proof, or
   - A citation to an independently vetted alternative source (e.g., Robert's "Selfless C*-algebras", Popa's original ultrapower results, or other peer-reviewed sources).
3. Do not paraphrase or restate a Jabbari result as if it were standard knowledge — the underlying claim itself is in dispute.

**Scope:** This instruction is global and applies to every proof attempt, revision, and decomposition step in this project.
